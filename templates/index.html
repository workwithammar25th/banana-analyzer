How it works

01
Upload
Drop any banana photo. One banana, a bunch, a fruit bowl — the model handles it all.
02
Feature Extraction
We convert the image to HSV colour space and extract yellow, green, brown and dark pixel ratios plus overall brightness.
03
Classification
A Random Forest classifier trained on 2,000 samples predicts the freshness stage for each detected banana region.
04
Shelf Life
A second Random Forest regressor estimates days remaining, with a mean absolute error of just 0.55 days.

Freshness stages

Fresh
5 – 8 days remaining
Green-yellow colouration, firm, high brightness. Will ripen further. Safe to eat or store.
Ripe
2 – 4 days remaining
Bright uniform yellow, high saturation. Maximum sweetness. Ideal window for consumption.
Overripe
0 – 1 days remaining
Brown patches developing, softening flesh, reduced brightness. Edible but best used for baking.
Rotten
0 days remaining
Dominated by dark pixels, very low brightness, significant decay. Not safe for consumption. Discard.
